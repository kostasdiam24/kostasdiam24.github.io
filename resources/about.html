A few words about me...

Hi, I'm Konstantinos Diamantopoulos, a 15-year-old web developer from Greece. I got into web development because I had a lot of free time and loved creating things. Plus, I found out that it's pretty easy and quick to learn. What really pulled me in was the idea of working remotely and earning enough to live comfortably.

I’ve learned HTML, CSS, and some JavaScript through Codecademy, which has been awesome. I have a few certificates that show what I’ve learned so far. I'm still new to this, so I don’t specialize in any particular projects yet, but I'm always excited to learn more and take on new challenges.

I don’t have any professional experience just yet, but I’ve been working on my own projects at home. I’ve made clones of Spotify and YouTube, along with a few other cool projects. These have been great practice and helped me get better at coding.

For me, web development is a way to live a financially free life and travel the world. I also love helping people create their own websites, especially since not everyone knows how to code. One of my big goals was to learn JavaScript by the end of the year, and I’m happy to say I’ve done that, even though I’m still learning more advanced stuff. The best part about being a web developer is that I can work whenever and wherever I want.

Location: Patra, Greece
